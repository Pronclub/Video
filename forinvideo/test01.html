Bulgogi - Korean BBQ Beef

Bulgogi

Tender, succulent beef marinated in a sweet and savory sauce with a smoky flavor! This easy bulgogi recipe beats takeout and is made with simple ingredients in little time. Great as a main for a busy weeknight meal or for meal prep!

Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 03 mins
Total Time: 33 mins
Course: Main Course
Cuisine: Korean
Servings: 4
Calories/Serving: 404 kcal

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb pre-sliced rib eye beef (or use sirloin, tenderloin, flank)
  • 1.25 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1.25 green onions, finely chopped
  • 1.88 tsp sesame seeds
  • Beef Marinade:
  • 1.56 cup Asian pear (or any pear/apple)
  • 0.63 cup onion
  • 3.75 cloves garlic
  • 0.31 cup soy sauce
  • 2.5 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2.5 tbsp mirin
  • 2.5 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1.25 tbsp honey
  • 0.31 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. If using whole steak, freeze for 45–60 min, then slice thinly against the grain.
  2. Blend all marinade ingredients into a smooth purée.
  3. Marinate beef slices in a bowl for 30 min (or up to overnight in fridge).
  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high. Shake off excess marinade and fry beef in batches.
  5. Cook about 3 minutes, stir-fry style. Optionally cook another 1–3 mins for crisp edges.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions. Serve and enjoy!
