Bulgogi – Korean BBQ Beef

Bulgogi (Korean BBQ Beef)

Tender, succulent beef marinated in a sweet and savory sauce with a smoky flavor! This easy bulgogi recipe beats takeout and is perfect for weeknight meals.

By Crimson Kitchen Published July 10, 2025
1 min read

Quick Info

  • Course: Main Course
  • Cuisine: Korean
  • Servings: 4
  • Calories: 404 kcal per serving
  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 3 mins
  • Total Time: 33 mins

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lb pre-sliced rib eye beef (or use Top Sirloin, Tenderloin, or Flank steak)
  • 1.25 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1.25 green onion, finely chopped (for garnish)
  • 1.88 tsp sesame seeds (for garnish)

Beef Marinade

  • 1.56 cup Asian Pear (or any pear/apple), peeled
  • 0.63 cup onion, peeled
  • 3.75 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 0.31 cup regular soy sauce
  • 2.5 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2.5 tbsp mirin
  • 2.5 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1.25 tbsp honey
  • 0.31 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. If using a whole steak, freeze for 45–60 mins, then thinly slice against the grain.
  2. Blend all marinade ingredients until smooth. (Or finely grate and mix by hand.)
  3. Marinate beef slices for 30 mins at room temp, or up to overnight in the fridge.
  4. Heat oil in pan on medium-high. Shake off excess marinade and fry beef in small batches.
  5. Cook for 3 mins (or up to 6 mins for browned edges), stir-frying as needed.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions. Serve hot!
